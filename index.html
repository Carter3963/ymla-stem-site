Courses Offered

  • Honors 6th-8th Grade Integrated Science: An advanced exploration of Earth, life, and physical sciences through inquiry, labs, and real-world problem solving.
  • Honors Biology: A rigorous life science course covering cell biology, genetics, evolution, and ecosystems using labs and modeling.
  • Honors Chemistry: Focuses on the properties and behaviors of matter, chemical reactions, and atomic structure with lab investigations.
  • Anatomy and Physiology: Introduces students to the structure and function of the human body, preparing them for health science pathways.
  • Physics & Environmental Systems: Combines fundamental physics concepts with environmental science to understand energy, motion, and sustainability.
  • Honors Mathematics (6th–Algebra I, Geometry): Offers accelerated instruction in middle school math, preparing students for advanced high school coursework.
